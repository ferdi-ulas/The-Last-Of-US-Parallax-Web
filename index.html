Joel lost his daughter years ago and continues his struggle to survive. One day, he is assigned to take Ellie, a little girl, from a group of rebels trying to survive in a post-apocalyptic world as a result of a worldwide epidemic. Ellie is one of the few people who are resistant to this epidemic thanks to her immunity, so Joel decides to take her to a safe place, to a group that can develop a vaccine. Joel and Ellie embark on a challenging journey and fight against countless dangers, infected creatures and traps created by humans on the way. As Joel takes on the responsibility of protecting Ellie, a strong bond develops between the two. However, the painful pasts experienced by both characters deepen the story in the process. After many dangers, Joel and Ellie reach a hospital where Ellie will undergo surgery in order to be used for the vaccine. However, Joel realizes that he cannot bear to lose Ellie and kills the hospital staff and kidnaps Ellie in order to save her. This decision leads Joel to conflict with his own conscience, because in order to survive in this world, sometimes it is necessary to question how much harm a person can do to others.
